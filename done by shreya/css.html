About Nepal.

Nepal, a picturesque country nestled in the heart of the Himalayas, captivates visitors with its breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and warm hospitality. From the snow-capped peaks of the world's highest mountains to the lush valleys and dense forests, Nepal's geography offers a diverse range of natural wonders. The country's spiritual essence is reflected in its numerous temples, monasteries, and sacred sites, where Hinduism and Buddhism coexist harmoniously.
